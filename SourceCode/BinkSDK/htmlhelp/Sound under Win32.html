Sound under Win32

Discussion

For sound support under Windows, Bink includes drivers for the Miles Sound System and DirectSound. See the descriptions of BinkSoundUseMiles and BinkSoundUseDirectSound for details on sound integration. In general, you will simply have to call one of these two functions to start playing Bink sound - everything else is handled automatically.
