Distributing the Bink DLL under Win32

Discussion

To distribute an application that uses Bink, you must include the Bink DLL (BinkW32.DLL) in the same directory as your application.
Do not place the BinkW32.DLL in the Windows or the Windows System directories! The BinkW32.DLL must be placed in your application directory!
